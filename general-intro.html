Global Intoduction

From multiple internship experiences and my undergraduate education, I have developed a strong interest in designing sustainable city infrastructures through automation, numerical modelling and data analysis. I believe well-designed sustainable infrastructure is essential to combat climate change and protect our society and the environment. Also, by leveraging the power of advanced computer technology, more effective and holistic solutions can be developed.

In my future career, I hope to design and build a greener and more resilient city by:

  1. Applying the design principles of sustainable development;
  2. Automating repetitive tasks in the design process, thus more resources can be allocated into research and development;
  3. Applying numerical modelling and data analysis to increase the accuracy of the analysis.

The following portfolio items are some of the project experiences I had in the past few years, and they all demonstrate my abilities in analysis, programming, research, and technical communication.
